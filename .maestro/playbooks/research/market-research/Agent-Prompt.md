## Description

Research the emerging landscape of **AI Buyer Agents and Autonomous Procurement** — AI-driven systems that select, negotiate for, and/or execute purchases on behalf of users or organizations.

### Scope

Cover all major subfamilies:
- **Enterprise procurement agents** (source-to-pay AI embedded in spend management suites)
- **Consumer shopping agents** (delegated checkout, price tracking, auto-buy)
- **Trading and investment buyer agents** (market execution bots, crypto, equities)
- **Negotiation buyer agents** (alternating offers, auction protocols, mediated mechanisms)
- **Payment-capable agents and commerce protocols** (AP2, UCP, ACP, Visa Intelligent Commerce, Mastercard Agent Pay)

### Key Research Questions
1. Who are the major commercial and open-source players?
2. What protocols and standards govern agent-to-merchant payment flows?
3. Which behavioral biases are most exploitable in current buyer agents?
4. What security and compliance gaps exist (PCI DSS, EMV 3DS, tokenization)?
5. What evaluation frameworks exist for measuring agent procurement quality?
